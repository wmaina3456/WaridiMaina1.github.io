Study Planner – Weekly Schedule

This webpage is a streamlined study planner designed for a Business Economics student. It lays out my weekly routine from Monday to Friday, with each day focused on something specific—lectures and readings on Mondays, problem sets on Tuesdays, macro review on Wednesdays, tutorials and note revision on Thursdays, and practice quizzes on Fridays. Saturdays are reserved strictly for assignments, completed between 10:00 a.m. and 3:00 p.m., so everything stays on track. A goals section highlights three priorities each week, such as improving Excel skills, finishing readings early, or preparing ahead for midterms. The simple layout helps me stay organized, manage my workload, and keep my semester running smoothly.

Technologies: HTML, basic CSS
